# Data Science Job Market Analysis
Tools: Excel | Tableau

## Key Findings

1. **Management roles command a 38% salary premium** — DS Managers 
   earn an average of $86.8K vs Data Analysts at $62.6K, 
   the lowest-paid category.

2. **Real Estate is the highest-paying industry** — offering an 
   average salary of $103.5K, above Finance and Technology sectors.

3. **Bellevue, Lake Forest and Mountain View are the top-paying 
   cities** — averaging $184.5K, $179.5K and $177.2K respectively, 
   well above the national dataset average.

4. **Most data science roles fall in the $50–75K salary band** — 
   suggesting mid-range compensation dominates the market with 
   fewer roles at the extremes.

5. **Large companies (5,000–10,000 employees) pay the most** — 
   indicating that mid-to-large enterprises offer the strongest 
   compensation packages for data scientists.
